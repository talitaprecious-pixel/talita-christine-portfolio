About Me

I am a Junior Developer at Abantubo Safaris, where I design and build practical digital solutions that support internal operations and decision-making. One of my key contributions has been developing an integrated internal system for tour operations, revenue analytics, and configurable field intelligence management. This project reflects my passion for building software that solves real business problems and improves efficiency.

Alongside my current role, I am deeply committed to growing as a software engineer. I have developed skills in Java, C#, PHP, JavaScript, Python, SQL, XAML, XML, and modern web technologies. I enjoy working on systems that connect technology with business needs, whether through web applications, database-driven solutions, or process automation tools.

Beyond tech, I am also an entrepreneur and the founder of East African Naturals, a brand focused on authentic natural hair and skincare solutions. My journey blends innovation, creativity, and leadership, and I am always looking for ways to create impact through both business and software.

I believe technology is most powerful when it is purposeful. Whether I am building systems, learning new frameworks, or growing a brand, I approach each challenge with curiosity, resilience, and a strong desire to keep evolving.
