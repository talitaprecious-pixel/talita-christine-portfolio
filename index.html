About Me

My journey in technology and entrepreneurship

I am Talita Christine (also known online as Talita Queen Precious), a dynamic entrepreneur, junior software developer, and lifelong learner based in Kampala, Uganda. As the founder and CEO of East African Naturals, I've built a brand dedicated to authentic, natural hair and skincare solutions rooted in wellness and sustainability.

Alongside entrepreneurship, I actively grow my skills in software development, working with C#, .NET, Java, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, SQL Server, and MySQL. I enjoy designing and building applications such as a Hostel Management System, POS concepts, and other practical projects that connect user needs, clean design, and solid backend logic.

My journey in technology began with a fascination for problem-solving and mathematics, which naturally evolved into software development. I believe that blending technology with entrepreneurship unlocks innovative ways to serve clients, automate processes, and create meaningful digital products for people and businesses.
